Our Preparation Process

At NewSeeds, we take pride in preparing every

dish with the utmost care, using fresh, high-quality ingredients.

Here's a glimpse into our kitchen process that makes your dining

experience truly special:

1. Sourcing the Best Ingredients

We partner with local farmers and suppliers to ensure our produce

is always fresh, organic, and sustainably grown. From vibrant vegetables

to nutrient-packed grains, we carefully select every ingredient to align

with our commitment to quality and environmental responsibility.

2. Made from Scratch

Every item on our menu is prepared in-house daily. From kneading dough for our

burger buns to blending creamy cashew-based sauces, we focus on

creating wholesome flavors without shortcuts or preservatives.

3. Thoughtful Preparation

Appetizers & Salads:

We wash, chop, and assemble our greens and veggies fresh every morning,

ensuring crisp textures and vibrant flavors in every bite.

Soups & Mains:

Our soups simmer slowly to build rich, deep flavors. For mains, ingredients

are prepared in small batches to ensure freshness, whether it's

grilling Beyond Meat patties to order or sautéing veggies for our buddha bowls.

Desserts:

Our desserts are crafted with love, using plant-based alternatives like

coconut cream, almond flour, and organic sweeteners to create indulgent yet guilt-free treats.

4. Minimal Waste, Maximum Sustainability

We strive to minimize waste in our kitchen. Leftover vegetable scraps are composted,

and we use eco-friendly packaging for takeout and delivery.

Sustainability isn't just a buzzword—it's our way of life.

5. A Dash of Love

Every dish is assembled with care, ensuring it looks as good as it tastes.

From the perfect swirl of pesto on your pasta to the delicate garnish

on your dessert, we believe presentation is part of the experience.
